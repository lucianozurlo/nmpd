About us NMP.D is an industrial design agency located in Buenos Aires, Argentina, specializing in consumer electronics, small appliances, and health and beauty equipment. We also specialize in creating high-quality visualizations and animations. Our main goal is to develop high-quality, meaningful products, focusing on user-centered design and user experience. NMP.D was born from the combined efforts and experience of two friends from the Universidad de Buenos Aires, Ignacio and Ramiro, who built solid careers at different companies and design agencies. They forged their own identity and a versatile style that allows them to assist large companies, startups, and studios, offering fast, agile, and top-quality service.
