Kali ®

Kali ®

2021

This 1-liter, double-walled stainless steel bottle was designed with a minimalist aesthetic. Its unique shape features a squircle-to-round transition and a perfect alignment between the body and cap.

A key design feature is the hygienic spout, which prevents users from making contact with the drinking surface while opening the bottle. Both the spout and the handle protrude minimally from the cap, preserving the bottle's sleek and understated profile.
