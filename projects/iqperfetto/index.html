IQ
Perfetto ®

Hair dryer
ga.ma Italy ®

2019

This professional hairdryer was designed to be the lightest on the market, directly challenging the high-end dominance of the Dyson Supersonic. Our design prioritized an eye-catching yet minimalist aesthetic with the smallest possible footprint.

The unique form factor was a direct result of seamless collaboration with our electronics engineering team, leveraging airflow to cool the microcontroller efficiently.

It's unique design established a new product category called high-speed hairdryers. Many came after inspiring on its form factor and design details.
