Pilltrack ®

Medication tracking device
Athelas ®

2021

PillTrack was a product designed to help patients maintain medication adherence. Both doctors and patients could view compliance rates through their respective online portals. It supported patients in tracking their medication doses via text reminders and intuitive light indicators.

We were hired by Athelas's engineering team to bring their idea to fruition, aiming to create a product that was attractive and modern, yet discreet for use in an elder care environment.

Pilltrack ®

Medication tracking device
Athelas ®

2021

LED Indicator

The device has an LED indicator for each pill bottle, working between red and blue, showing which medication has been taken (blue) or not (red).

If the patient is ever late to take a dose, the PillTrack will help them by sending a reminder notification to their mobile phone.
